C4 - Keeping up with Meditation

This one's about sticking to meditation

What did you learn from your mindfulness conversation? How did the other person find it?

That it's very much outside of the way I would normally carry a conversation. It's much more attention-driven, where it's not about just hearing a story, it's also about creating a full awareness of the meaning behind what you're hearing rather than having a knee-jerk response to what's being said. The other person was Reuben, if memory serves, he mentioned how looping was a nice practice to ensure that both parties get their thoughts across accurately and vividly.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

I think i'll try to do a bit of everything. Having a buddy sounds great and I have a few candidates i'd like to proposition for practice. Regular training below your threshold, I think I already enjoy doing that. XD
Also, i'm already chill, so remembering to do just any amount of meditation will work out pretty well for me.

Any other musings?

I liked the analogy concerning babies that are learning to walk. It's always nice to know when you're training in a skill that may not produce very apparent results immediately.
